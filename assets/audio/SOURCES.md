# Audio sources

Both files derive from CC0 (public domain) recordings. No attribution is legally
required; recorded here for provenance.

Assets ship as AAC (.m4a): raw PCM WAV decoded as static via just_audio/AVPlayer on
iOS 26, so keep bundled sounds in a compressed container.

- `bell.m4a` — the final strike (cut at 0.47s) of "Boxing bell #1" from
  bigsoundbank.com (sound #1926, CC0), recorded on a real boxing bell with a
  Neumann KM184 / SoundDevices MixPre-3 at 48kHz/24-bit. Peak-normalized to
  -1 dBFS, 8ms fade-in, 1s fade-out, 4.2s total.
  https://bigsoundbank.com/boxing-bell-1-s1926.html
  (Replaced an earlier vintage optical-film transfer that carried hiss and a
  slate announcement in the tail.)
- `clapper.m4a` — three repeats of "Wood block hit" by thomasjaunism,
  https://freesound.org/people/thomasjaunism/sounds/218460/ (CC0), 280ms onset
  spacing, peak-normalized.
