## Description

**The round timer that works like the one on your gym wall.**

Giant LED digits. A real boxing bell. The 10-second clapper. Amber, green,
red — get ready, fight, rest. One tap and you're training.

**Built for fighters:**
• Boxing (pro & amateur), MMA, Muay Thai, Judo, BJJ, Wrestling — exact rules, no setup
• Grappling done right: real Judo and BJJ round formats, not an afterthought
• Conditioning built in: Tabata, HIIT 30/30, EMOM, or build any custom interval

**It never lets you down mid-round:**
• Bell rings with the screen locked and the phone on the gym floor
• Round + countdown on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island
• Your music keeps playing — bells ring over it, never pause it
• Drift-free timing, verified to the second
• Rotate for wall-timer mode: digits readable across the gym
• Whole screen is the pause button — works with wraps and gloves on

**And nothing you don't want:**
• Free forever. No ads. No account. No subscription. No tracking.
• Tiny download, works completely offline
